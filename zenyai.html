ZENYAI web design

Atom   |   HTML   |   CSS   |   Bootstrap   |   Illustrator   |   Sketch   |   InVision   |  Git&GitHub

Rebranding authentic Thai restaurant. Traditional Thai dishes, soups & street food.

PROJECT OVERVIEW

Serving food in a charming, old fashion Thai style.

Zen Yai Thai Restaurant has been serving the customers since 1973. And the also well known between Thai-communities in the United States as Thai traditional restaurant. All the Thai-American have to visit if come to San Francisco.

PROJECT GOAL

“Rebranding ZenYai Thai restaurant to be a contemporary style for expanding the target customers to others not just Thai and Asian.”

BUSINESS ANALYSIS

The food prices are affordable. Some menu prices low as $2.5 for a small portion, but still the great quality.

The other special is many Thai restaurants in the states are usually ignored using the right ingredient. For example, basil leaf. They are usually using sweet basil leaf because a long time kept, and less expensive. But the Zen Yai Thai Restaurant using all exact ingredients and cook the way authentic Thai's taste.

The target market is Thai or Asian who loves authentic spicy food. Big or small pocket are welcome if you are starving and real Thai taste addicted. Do not surprise if you see the special of the day on the chock board menu written in the Thai language that because it's special.

PITCH

"If you miss Thailand, you have to eat the Thai food. But if you miss the Thai food, you have to go to Zen Yai Thai Restaurant."

MISSION STATEMENT

Clandestino uses podcast, video, and blog as their main source to reach the community.

Clandestino was founded by José in 2012.

Clandestino’s goal is to by, entertaining, educating, and informing the community, establish new ways of communication and understanding among cultures.

This will be accomplished by sharing social memes (in music, arts, and social issues) stemming from different cultures and parts of the world, offering a spotlight to different perspectives.

PROJECT GOAL

“Rebranding Clandestino radio to expanding and sharing more contents in arts and entertainment.”

TARGET AUDIENCE

Multicultural young adult, who tries to make a change in the world; anyone looking to discover new topics and unusual meaningful stories.
