i-tone Full-Stack

Atom   |   HTML   |   CSS   |   Python   |   JavaScript   |   SQLite   |   Bootstrap   |   Django   |   Illustrator   |   Sketch   |   InVision   |   GitHub

Emotion Tracking Web App. The visual mood tracking journal and diary application, which helps you understand your emotions and triggers. Anywhere, anytimes. The better know your emotions.

PROJECT OVERVIEW

Improve your strength by leading your emotions.

Journaling is an incredible stress management tool, a good habit that mitigates the impact of physical stressors on your health. Also, a journal helps to clarify our goals and helps to solve the problems of the past.

i-tone is a visual mood tracking journal and diary application, which helps you understand your emotions and trigger.

i-tone provides several options to help the user understand the influence of people, surroundings, environment, time and other factors that impact mood which are called triggers.

PROBLEM STATEMENT

“How might we help veteran and service members who are diagnosed with PTSD, journal their emotions to understand the problems and triggers easier?”

SOLUTION

Build the one stop learning application that is for crypto enthusiasts of a beginner level.

The courses are directed at beginners who are looking to scratch the surface of crypto investing. It will teach you from introduction to cryptocurrency, to do things like set up the trading account on any platform, or deposit coins into exchanges with Cryptocurrency specialist volunteer without fee of any charge.

The user is able to contact with the cryptocurrency specialist volunteer for the individual learning perspective in your area to build the great Cryptocurrency community.

TARGET AUDIENCE

All gender

Age between 15-70 years old

Low to High income

Who are the little or no technological background.

Want to get start to learn blockchain technology and cryptocurrency.

The beginners who are looking to scratch the surface of crypto investing.

SECONDARY SOURCING

A survey covering a number of different questions surrounding cryptocurrency.

According to Social trading firm eToro survey, the firm released the results of a survey covering a number of different questions surrounding cryptocurrency. The survey, which was carried out by eToro’s US division, suggests that there is a shortage of decent cryptocurrency educational materials on offer.

69 % of the respondents are interested in learning more about digital currency.

75 % of those who didn’t hold any cryptocurrency to their names said they weren’t knowledgeable about it.

50 % of the people surveyed said that their lack of knowledge was preventing them from investing appropriately in the asset class.

97 % of millennial respondents who had already invested in crypto said they were interested in learning more.

74 % of women who took part in the survey said that they didn’t possess enough cryptocurrency knowledge.

Survey Finding

It’s important to acknowledge something. Several people have confessed that their general lack of knowledge is preventing them from investing, but having this knowledge isn’t necessarily going to convince them that investing is the best way to go. While classes and education are important, they won’t necessarily lead to an influx of investors.
