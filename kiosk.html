SIGN-IN KIOSK UI/UX

Atom   |   HTML   |   CSS   |   Bootstrap   |   Illustrator   |   Sketch   |   InVision   |  Git&GitHub

The Kiosk that helps the Mac Lab staff manage students’ ID and seats.

PROJECT OVERVIEW

Change the current check-in system to the digital system to make the front desk staffs work more efficient.

Using the student ID to check-in at Mac Lab at the Academy of Art University is for safety concerns and also to make sure the student who’s using it has authority to use it.

Therefore, The student’s ID card management and front desk check-in system are important to the service side and also convenience to the student.

PROBLEM STATEMENT

However, during peak time, the front desk staff feel very stressed and overwhelmed when students check-in and out at the same time, the current system makes the process very repeatable and inconvenient.

Besides, staff have to exchange the ID with the seat number back and forth every time which the staff complain about a lot.

We want to change the Mac Lab’s current check-in system to the digital system to make the front desk staff work more efficient.

“How might we help the Mac Lab front desk staffs manage students’ ID and seat number more efficiently when check-in?”

EVALUATION

Idea I: Innovation Solution

We release the new students' ID card. The seat number will be displayed on the card after check-in at the kiosk. Also, the student information will be displayed on the staff side.

This solution would help them reduce the time in explaining to the students about the seat location. Also, they do not need the seat card anymore.

Idea II: Effective Solution

In order to help staff easily manage the seats, this idea is to visualize the seat number and room layout on the digital screen.

After the students selected the seat, the kiosk will give the seat card to student and students will insert the card back into the kiosk when checking-out.

This solution will reduce the processing time for staff in exchange for the student’s ID with the seat card.

SOLUTION

By combining ID scanning ideal and visualizing the seat ideal into the Kiosk design system.

Based on the interview with staff. Their pain point is about having a hard time to exchange student ID and seat card every time for both check-in and check-out, especially during the peak time.

Our solution is to help the staff save time on assigning seats for students, reduce unnecessary check-in steps and make the check-in process more efficient and official.

SECONDARY SOURCING

A survey covering a number of different questions surrounding cryptocurrency.

According to Social trading firm eToro survey, the firm released the results of a survey covering a number of different questions surrounding cryptocurrency. The survey, which was carried out by eToro’s US division, suggests that there is a shortage of decent cryptocurrency educational materials on offer.

69 % of the respondents are interested in learning more about digital currency.

75 % of those who didn’t hold any cryptocurrency to their names said they weren’t knowledgeable about it.

50 % of the people surveyed said that their lack of knowledge was preventing them from investing appropriately in the asset class.

97 % of millennial respondents who had already invested in crypto said they were interested in learning more.

74 % of women who took part in the survey said that they didn’t possess enough cryptocurrency knowledge.

Survey Finding

It’s important to acknowledge something. Several people have confessed that their general lack of knowledge is preventing them from investing, but having this knowledge isn’t necessarily going to convince them that investing is the best way to go. While classes and education are important, they won’t necessarily lead to an influx of investors.
